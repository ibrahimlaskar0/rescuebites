About us

Rescue Bites is a platform dedicated to reducing food waste by connecting restaurants and food donors with NGOs and organizations that distribute surplus food to those in need. Our mission is to create a sustainable food ecosystem where no edible food goes to waste, ensuring it reaches the hungry and stray animals.
